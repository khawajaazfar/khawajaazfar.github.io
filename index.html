About Me

I am a Software Engineer with a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering from Lahore Garrison University,

My undergraduate thesis was Dental Enumeration and Diseases Detection on Panoramic X-Rays try it out using YOLOv8, YOLOv12 (it will take time in loading) a deep learning based model for analysis of Panoramic X-Rays. During my undergraduate studies, I had the privilege of working under the supervision of Prof. Rafaqat Alam Khan. My research interests including Machine Learning, Multimodal LLMs, Computer Vision, Medical Image Analysis and AI in healthcare which provided me with a strong foundation in Skills Gained.
